Clues Handbook
NOTE: This is a fast reference to clues, to better understand the all game please complete the start pack.
Left To Column Clue
The symbol means "immediately to the left of". For example the clue means that is immediately to the left of
Next To Column Clue
The symbol means "next to". For example the clue means that is immediately to the the left OR right of and vice-versa.
Same Column Clue
The symbol means "at the same column". For example the clue means that and are at the same grid column.
Middle Clues
The symbol means "in the middle". For example the clue would mean that is in the middle columns of the grid.
