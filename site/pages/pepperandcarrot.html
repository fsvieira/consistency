Pepper & Carrot (Theme)

Pepper & Carrot is made by David Revoy and its a free, libre and open-source webcomic supported directly by its patrons to change the comic book industry!

Because I like the all project idea and love the drawings, I decided to make a Pepper & Carrot theme for the consistency game.
