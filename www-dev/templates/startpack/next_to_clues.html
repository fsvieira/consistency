A next to column column clue as the symbol and it means "next to". For example the clue means that is immediately to the left OR right of and vice-versa.
This is one of the most tricky clue in the game, because it tells us that two items are next to each other but it does not tell their order.
Example
In a grid 2x2 consider the clue
,
the face may be at left column of the orange,
or it can be at the right,
In a grid 3x2, illustrating the same clue possibilities would look something like this:
If face is in the middle then orange may be in the left column or the right column,
If orange is in the middle then face may be in the left column or the right column,
