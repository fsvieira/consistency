This puzzle only have 'Middle Clues'...
A middle clue as the symbol and means "in the middle". For example the clue means that this face is in the middle columns of the grid (on line face category).
Example
In this puzzle we have a grid of 3x3 and middle clues,
tell us that each clue item is in the 2nd column of its line category,
On a puzzle of 4x3, middle clues tell us that the clue item is at 2nd OR 3rd column of its line category,
So middle clues means that the item is never at the first and last column of its line category.
